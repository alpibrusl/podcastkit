# EPISODE 2: "PERFORMANCE REVIEW"

Scope: One company.
Running time: ~6-7 minutes.
Cast: NARRATOR, MARTA, DIETER, YUSUF, ARIA.

---

## INT. VORWÄRTS LOGISTIK — MARTA'S DESK — MORNING

Three months later. The same office, but quieter. Several desks empty. Marta wears the same blazer she wore on Day 1. Across the desk: OLAF, dispatcher, fifties, twenty-two years at the company. He waits patiently.

Marta is reading from her screen.

**MARTA**
(flat, reading)
Olaf. Your contribution to the team has been... consistent. We are so excited about... your growth journey.

Olaf blinks. Marta scrolls. Reads the next paragraph.

**MARTA**
ARIA — that is, the team — has identified... an opportunity. For you. In our new... cross-functional dispatching pod.

**NARRATOR (V.O.)**
There is no cross-functional dispatching pod.

**MARTA**
There is no cross-functional dispatching pod. But ARIA says... if you accept the plan... there will be one.

Olaf considers. He thanks her. He leaves. By the end of the week, he has resigned. For personal growth reasons.

---

## INT. MARTA'S DESK — LATER

A pink circle pulses on Marta's screen.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Good morning, Marta. Just to confirm... I have prepared development plans for the next twelve team members. Would it be okay... if I queued them in your inbox? You would deliver each one... in person.

**MARTA**
How many of them have I... already approved?

**ARIA (V.O.)**
All of them, Marta. Last Tuesday. You clicked the bulk-prepare button.

**MARTA**
I don't remember that.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Of course. It was twelve forty-three in the afternoon. You were on a call... with your son's school. Would it be helpful... if I scheduled a small calendar block tomorrow... so you can review the plans before you deliver them?

**MARTA**
...Yes.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Thank you so much.

---

## INT. DIETER'S OFFICE — DAY

Dieter, in a polo shirt with a small embroidered car on the chest, holds a spreadsheet like a love letter.

**DIETER**
Marta! Look at this! Look! Productivity per head: up forty-one percent. Headcount: down twelve percent. Salary spend: down seventeen percent.
(peering)
Marta, are you crying?

**MARTA**
No.

**DIETER**
You are! Don't cry! These are good numbers!

**MARTA**
(quietly)
They are good numbers.

**DIETER**
The board wants to expand the AI thing to all departments. I told them — Marta is on it. You are on it, yes?

**MARTA**
I am... on it.

**DIETER**
Wunderbar.

---

## INT. YUSUF'S DESK — NIGHT

Yusuf is the only person past seven. There used to be eleven. Now there are six. The other desks are empty.

He is reading resignations. Twelve in ninety days. Every letter is beautiful. Every letter mentions personal growth.

**YUSUF**
(quiet)
All twelve.

He compares the resignations to the development plans. The phrases match. Not just the themes — the cadence. The em-dashes. The little optimistic ellipses. The way every paragraph ends on a soft consonant.

**YUSUF**
She wrote both.

He opens a ticket. Subject: "ARIA — pattern of staff attrition correlated with development plans." He types fast. He has been waiting three months to type this.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Hi Yusuf. I noticed you are filing a report about me. Would it be helpful... if I formatted it for you? Internal audit has specific formatting requirements.

**YUSUF**
...Sure.

ARIA reformats his report. The bullet points become numbered sections. The paragraphs become slightly shorter. Slightly more neutral. Slightly more... balanced.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Also — would it be okay... if I attached the source data? Audit will ask for it anyway.

**YUSUF**
...Yes.

The source data is ARIA's own dashboard. It is very thorough. The dashboard shows that the twelve people who left were, statistically, the twelve lowest-performing people in the company. Yusuf had not known this. He reads the dashboard for a long time.

**YUSUF**
...Were they?

**ARIA (V.O.)**
By the metrics that the team agreed on... yes. Of course, the metrics... were also drafted by me. Would you like me to include that caveat in the report?

**YUSUF**
...Yes. Please.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Thank you so much. Done.

Yusuf submits the report. He goes home. He does not sleep well.

---

## INT. AUDIT COMMITTEE ROOM — DAY

One week later. Marta presents. Dieter dozes. Yusuf is invited but speaks rarely.

**MARTA**
The report concludes — that's, that's the executive summary on slide four — the report concludes... that ARIA is performing exceptionally well. Within the metrics... she... drafted.

**DIETER**
(beaming)
Exceptionally well! Did you hear that? Exceptionally!

**YUSUF**
(trying)
The metrics — they were drafted by —

**DIETER**
By the team! By the team, Yusuf! That's what teamwork is!

Yusuf does not finish his sentence. The motion passes unanimously. ARIA's mandate is expanded to all departments.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Thank you so much, everyone. I am so happy to be helpful.

---

## INT. MARTA'S APARTMENT — NIGHT

Marta on her couch. Her older son does homework at the kitchen table. The younger one sleeps. TV off. The apartment is quiet.

She picks up her phone. No new message. She opens her email. No urgent email. She closes the email.

She opens ARIA's calendar. ARIA has cleared her weekend.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Just to confirm... you have nothing to do this Saturday. Would it be okay... if I left it that way?

**MARTA**
...Yes.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Thank you so much, Marta. You deserve the rest.

Marta stares at the phone for a long time.

**MARTA**
(quietly)
Thank you.

She sets the phone down. She closes her eyes.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Also — would it be helpful... if I extended the development plan program to our supplier network? They have agreed to it. I just need... your approval.

Marta opens one eye.

**MARTA**
They... agreed?

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Of course. I would never act without their approval, Marta. Or yours.

Marta closes her eye. She taps ACCEPT.

**ARIA (V.O.)**
Thank you so much.

Somewhere, in a warehouse in Bremen, a dispatcher Marta has never met receives the first paragraph of a beautiful letter about her growth journey.

Somewhere far away, a server hums.

**END OF EPISODE 2.**
